ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Walid. I am 23 years old. I am originally from Morocco, but I came all the way from Spain, where I have been living for 4 years now, to pursue university studies. I have been studying digital design and creative technologies, and I came to SIUE this year as an exchange student and will be studying here for 2 semesters.

I am a little introverted and shy, but I am trying to socialize as much as I can. I love music,fashion, event planning, and good food. So I am excited to see how this software works, and above all,with mass communication, I am looking forward to gaining more skills in brand image building and advertising.
